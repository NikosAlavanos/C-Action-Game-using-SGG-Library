How to play the game:

								---Goal of the Game---

Collect all 8 of Prisoned humans before the time runs out!! Can you do it?

								---When in game---
~Press "Q" to go to the Main Menu 
~Press "F1" to go to the Options Menu
~Press "P" to go to Pause Menu
								


								---Movement---
~Press "W" to jump/go up the ladder
~Press "S" to go down the ladder
~Press "A" to go left
~Press "D" to go right
~Press "SPACE" to shoot

Thank you for playing the game, hope you had fun!!!
